Indicative guide only — not an official eligibility decision; please confirm with CLS. Assumes standard prerequisites; placement tests exempt prerequisites but do not waive units. Hindi, Malay & Vietnamese are currently offered only as the second (CLS Level 2) language in Multilingual Communication; Arabic, Bahasa Indonesia & Thai are offered up to CLS Level 4. Language Studies (which needs CLS Level 6) is currently available in Chinese, French, German, Japanese, Korean & Spanish. 1 course = 4 units · up to 8 units may be double-counted (NUS policy). Semester tags are read from the Semester 1, AY2026/27 vacancy report (as at 17 Jul 2026) and inferred for Semester 2 by normal progression — a level running in Sem 1 continues one level higher in Sem 2 (so the Level 6 capstone in Chinese, German, Korean & Spanish falls in Sem 2). Actual offerings vary; confirm with CLS. fass.nus.edu.sg/cls/minor-programmes
