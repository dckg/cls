Two Ways to Minor in Languages

Declare one — or both. You can hold both minors by double-counting up to 8 units between them.

Minor in Language Studies

Study one CLS language.

  • Pass 20 units from:
  • 5 CLS courses (20 units) up to at least CLS Level 6
  • OR 4 CLS courses (16 units) up to at least CLS Level 6 AND 1 CLS-recognised course (4 units)
Outcome

Single-language mastery & depth

Minor in Multilingual Communication New

Study two CLS languages.

  • Pass 20 units from:
  • 3 CLS courses (12 units) for one language offered up to at least CLS Level 4
  • AND 2 CLS courses (8 units) for another language offered up to at least CLS Level 2
Outcome

A broader language portfolio
